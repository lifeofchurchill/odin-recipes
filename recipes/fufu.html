Description

This is a Ghanaian delicacy called fufu. It is made from cassava and plantain.
the cassava and plantain are cooked and pounded to get the final product

Ingredients

  • Cassava
  • Plantain

Steps

  1. Peel the Cassava and plantain
  2. Cut them into smaller pieces
  3. put them in a pot and add water
  4. Boil for about 30 minutes max
  5. After boiling pound both cassava and plantain together
  6. after pounding till designed feel is gotten proceed to eat
